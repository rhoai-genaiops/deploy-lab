Graduate Learning Outcomes

Graduates will be able to:

1

Creative Expression

Develop original artistic concepts and express ideas through various traditional and digital media.

2

Technical Proficiency

Master fundamental and advanced techniques in drawing, painting, design, and digital art creation.

3

Critical Analysis

Analyze and critique artworks using art historical knowledge and contemporary theoretical frameworks.

4

Design Thinking

Apply design principles and problem-solving methodologies to create effective visual communications.

5

Digital Literacy

Use contemporary digital tools and technologies for artistic creation and design projects.

6

Professional Practice

Understand the art industry, including exhibition practices, marketing, and career development strategies.

7

Collaboration

Work effectively in creative teams and engage with diverse communities through art projects.

8

Cultural Awareness

Understand art's role in society and create work that reflects cultural sensitivity and global perspectives.
