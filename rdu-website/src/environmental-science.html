Graduate Learning Outcomes

Graduates will be able to:

1

Scientific Analysis

Apply scientific principles to analyze environmental problems and assess their impacts on ecosystems and human health.

2

Field Research

Conduct comprehensive field studies using appropriate sampling techniques and analytical instruments.

3

Data Analysis

Process and interpret environmental data using statistical methods, GIS, and modeling techniques.

4

Policy Analysis

Evaluate environmental policies and regulations, understanding their scientific basis and societal implications.

5

Sustainability Solutions

Develop and assess sustainable solutions to environmental challenges across multiple scales.

6

Communication

Communicate environmental science findings effectively to diverse stakeholders and the public.

7

Interdisciplinary Thinking

Integrate knowledge from multiple disciplines to address complex environmental problems.

8

Ethics & Responsibility

Apply ethical frameworks to environmental decision-making and demonstrate environmental stewardship.

Laboratories & Facilities

Environmental Laboratories

Water quality testing, soil analysis, air monitoring equipment, and analytical instrumentation for environmental samples.

Field Research Station

On-site research facility with meteorological station, stream monitoring, and ecosystem study plots.

GIS & Modeling Lab

Advanced computing facilities with ArcGIS, ENVI, R, and climate modeling software for spatial analysis.

Greenhouse & Growth Chambers

Controlled environment facilities for plant and ecosystem research, including climate manipulation experiments.

Capstone Design Examples

Urban Heat Island Mitigation

Assess urban heat island effects and design green infrastructure solutions for local communities.

Wetland Restoration Planning

Develop comprehensive restoration plans for degraded wetland ecosystems using ecological principles.

Water Quality Assessment

Monitor and analyze water quality in local watersheds, identifying pollution sources and mitigation strategies.

Carbon Footprint Analysis

Conduct life cycle assessments and develop carbon reduction strategies for organizations or communities.
