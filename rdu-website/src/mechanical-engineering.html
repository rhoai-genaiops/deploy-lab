Graduate Learning Outcomes

Graduates will be able to:

1

System Modeling

Model mechanical systems using first principles (Newton/Euler, conservation laws) and modern CAE tools.

2

Design Excellence

Design components and assemblies to meet functional, safety, and cost targets using DfX principles.

3

Analysis Methods

Analyze structural, thermal, and fluid phenomena via closed-form, numerical (FEM/CFD), and experimental methods.

4

Manufacturing & Validation

Manufacture & validate prototypes using conventional and digital fabrication; interpret GD&T and inspection data.

5

Control Systems

Control dynamic systems and integrate sensors/actuators with embedded control for mechatronic products.

6

Sustainability

Evaluate energy systems, life-cycle impacts, and sustainability metrics with appropriate standards.

7

Professional Communication

Communicate designs, risks, and decisions ethically and professionally; work effectively in teams.

8

Continuous Learning

Learn independently and adapt to evolving technologies and regulations.

Laboratories & Facilities

Thermofluids Lab

Wind tunnel, flow benches, heat exchanger rigs, pumps & fans, PIV demonstration systems.

Materials & Manufacturing

UTM, hardness testers, microscopes, furnaces, CNC mills/lathes, 3D printers (FDM/SLA/SLS), welding equipment.

Dynamics & Vibration

Shaker table, impact hammer, accelerometers, data acquisition systems for modal testing.

CAE & Computing

Workstations with SolidWorks/Inventor, ANSYS/Abaqus/CFX/Fluent, MATLAB/Python, version control systems.

Capstone Design Examples

Solar-Assisted Heat Pump

Performance modeling, refrigerant selection, heat exchanger design, control systems, life-cycle analysis, and regulatory compliance.

Autonomous Inspection Robot

Chassis & drivetrain design, sensing & control systems, safety interlocks, fleet coordination basics, manufacturability analysis.

High-Efficiency UAV Propeller

Aerodynamic optimization, composite manufacturing, structural testing, noise reduction, and performance validation.

Lightweight Composite Bicycle Frame

Laminate design, topology optimization, manufacturing processes, structural testing, and fatigue analysis.
