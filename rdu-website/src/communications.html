Graduate Learning Outcomes

Graduates will be able to:

1

Strategic Communication

Develop and implement strategic communication plans for diverse audiences and organizational goals.

2

Media Production

Create compelling content across multiple media platforms including video, audio, and digital formats.

3

Research & Analysis

Conduct communication research and analyze media effects using qualitative and quantitative methods.

4

Journalism Skills

Report, write, and edit news stories while adhering to journalistic standards and ethics.

5

Digital Literacy

Navigate and leverage digital technologies and social media platforms for effective communication.

6

Public Relations

Manage organizational reputation and relationships with stakeholders through strategic PR campaigns.

7

Critical Thinking

Analyze media messages critically and understand their impact on society and culture.

8

Professional Ethics

Apply ethical principles and legal considerations in all communication and media practices.
