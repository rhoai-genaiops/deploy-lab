Graduate Learning Outcomes

Graduates will be able to:

1

Global Analysis

Analyze complex international issues using theoretical frameworks and interdisciplinary approaches.

2

Cultural Competency

Demonstrate deep understanding of diverse cultures and their impact on international affairs.

3

Policy Analysis

Evaluate international policies and their effectiveness in addressing global challenges.

4

Research Skills

Conduct rigorous research on international topics using primary and secondary sources.

5

Language Proficiency

Communicate effectively in at least one foreign language at an advanced intermediate level.

6

Diplomatic Skills

Navigate cross-cultural communication and negotiation in international contexts.

7

Critical Thinking

Think critically about global events and their historical, political, and economic contexts.

8

Global Citizenship

Demonstrate ethical awareness and commitment to addressing global challenges collaboratively.
