Bachelor of Science in Business Administration

4 Years, 240 ECTS

A comprehensive business program combining management theory, analytical skills, and practical experience to prepare leaders for the modern business world.

📄 Download Program Info Sheet (PDF)

Program Overview

Award: B.Sc. in Business Administration
Duration: 8 Semesters (4 academic years)
Total Credits: 240 ECTS
Delivery: Lectures (L), Tutorials (T), Laboratories (P), Studio/Project (S), Fieldwork (F)
Workload: 1 ECTS ≈ 25–30 hours

Program Pillars

  • Strategic Management
  • Financial Analysis
  • Marketing & Sales
  • Operations Management
  • Human Resources
  • Business Analytics
  • Entrepreneurship
  • Global Business
  • Ethics & Leadership

Graduate Learning Outcomes

Graduates will be able to:

1

Strategic Thinking

Analyze business environments and develop strategic plans for organizational success and growth.

2

Financial Management

Evaluate financial performance, manage budgets, and make informed investment and financing decisions.

3

Leadership Skills

Lead teams effectively, manage change, and inspire others to achieve organizational objectives.

4

Data-Driven Decisions

Use business analytics and data science techniques to support evidence-based decision making.

5

Marketing Excellence

Develop and implement marketing strategies that create value for customers and stakeholders.

6

Global Perspective

Navigate international business environments and understand cross-cultural management practices.

7

Ethical Decision Making

Apply ethical principles and corporate social responsibility in business decision making.

8

Innovation & Entrepreneurship

Identify opportunities, develop business models, and create innovative solutions to market needs.

Laboratories & Facilities

Business Simulation Labs

Advanced computer labs with business simulation software for experiential learning and decision making.

Finance Trading Room

Real-time market data terminals with Bloomberg and Reuters for financial analysis and investment practice.

Case Study Rooms

Collaborative spaces designed for team-based learning, case analysis, and presentation preparation.

Business Incubator

Entrepreneurship center providing mentorship, resources, and workspace for student startup ventures.

Capstone Design Examples

Sustainable Business Model Innovation

Develop environmentally sustainable business models for traditional industries facing climate challenges.

Digital Transformation Strategy

Create comprehensive digital transformation plans for small-to-medium enterprises in various sectors.

Social Impact Venture

Design and launch a social enterprise that addresses community needs while generating sustainable revenue.
