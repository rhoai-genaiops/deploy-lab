Graduate Learning Outcomes

Graduates will be able to:

1

Statistical Analysis

Apply advanced statistical methods and probability theory to analyze data and test hypotheses.

2

Machine Learning

Design, implement, and evaluate machine learning models for classification, regression, and clustering tasks.

3

Programming Skills

Develop efficient algorithms and software solutions using Python, R, SQL, and other data science tools.

4

Data Management

Design and manage databases, handle big data systems, and ensure data quality and integrity.

5

Visualization & Communication

Create compelling data visualizations and communicate insights effectively to diverse audiences.

6

Problem Solving

Formulate data-driven solutions to complex real-world problems across various domains.

7

Ethics & Privacy

Address ethical considerations, privacy concerns, and bias issues in data science applications.

8

Research & Innovation

Conduct independent research and develop innovative approaches to data science challenges.
